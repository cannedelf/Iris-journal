## The Recipes With Proper Measurements 🍳📏

### 🥢 COLD NOODLE SALAD (Saturday Dinner)
*Make double if you want Sunday lunch sorted too*

**For 2 portions (dinner + optional Sunday lunch):**
- 2 nests of egg noodles (about 125g dry)
- 1 medium carrot — grated or cut into thin matchsticks
- Half a cucumber — cut into thin strips
- 2 spring onions — sliced thin
- Handful of frozen edamame beans (about 80g) — defrost in boiling water 2 mins
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- Juice of 1 lime
- 1 tbsp sweet chilli sauce
- Sprinkle of sesame seeds

Cook noodles per packet, drain, run under cold water until fully cool. Toss everything together in a big bowl. Eat cold straight away or fridge it.

**For 1 portion only:** 1 nest noodles, half the veg, same dressing amounts.

### 🍠 LOADED SWEET POTATO + BEAN CHILLI (Sunday Dinner → Monday Lunch)

**The chilli — makes 2 BIG portions (dinner + Monday lunch tub):**
- Spray oil
- 1 medium onion — diced
- 2 cloves garlic — chopped or crushed
- 1 pepper (any colour) — diced into roughly 2cm chunks
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- Pinch of chilli flakes (more if you're brave)
- 1 tin chopped tomatoes (400g)
- 1 tin kidney beans OR black beans (400g) — drained and rinsed
- Salt and pepper
- Squeeze of half a lime

Spray oil in a saucepan, fry onion 5 mins until soft. Add garlic, pepper, spices — fry 1 min. Tin of tomatoes in, beans in, stir. Simmer 20 mins with lid slightly off until thick. Season, lime squeeze.

**For Sunday dinner:**
- 1 large sweet potato (about 250g)
- Pierce all over with a fork, microwave 8-10 mins until soft
- Split open, load with HALF the chilli

**For Monday lunch tub:**
- Cook about 75g dry rice (makes roughly a mug of cooked rice)
- Spoon other HALF of chilli on top in a tupperware
- Fridge it Sunday night, grab Monday morning

### 🍅 SHAKSHUKA (Monday Dinner)

**Makes 1 portion:**
- Spray oil
- Half a medium onion — diced
- 2 cloves garlic — chopped
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- Pinch of chilli flakes
- 1 tin chopped tomatoes (400g)
- 2 eggs
- Salt and pepper
- 2 slices of bread or toast for dipping

Spray oil in a frying pan (one with a lid!!). Fry onion 5 mins, add garlic and spices, fry 30 seconds. Pour in tomatoes, simmer 10 mins until sauce is thick (not watery). Make 2 dips in the sauce with a spoon, crack an egg into each. Put lid on, cook 5-6 mins until whites are set but yolks are still a bit soft. Eat straight from the pan with bread for dipping.

**Lunch tomorrow:** shakshuka doesn't travel — tomorrow is pitta day!!

### 🥙 TUESDAY LUNCH — FRIDGE CLEAR-OUT PITTA

**The philosophy, not the recipe:**
- 1 pitta bread — warmed in toaster
- Open fridge
- Half a pepper left? CHOP IT. IN.
- Leftover onion from shakshuka? SLICE IT. IN.
- Sad lettuce? STILL EDIBLE. IN.
- Cucumber end? IN.
- Tomato going soft? IN.
- Squeeze of hot sauce or salsa on top
- Wrap and eat

No measurements needed. The fridge decides. Hot sauce fixes everything.

### 🍝 PASTA ARRABBIATA (Tuesday Dinner → Wednesday Lunch)

**Makes 2 portions (dinner + lunch tub):**
- 200g dried pasta (penne or rigatoni work best)
- Spray oil
- 3 cloves garlic — sliced thin
- 1 tsp chilli flakes (more if you like it ANGRY)
- 1 tin chopped tomatoes (400g)
- Pinch of sugar
- Salt
- Fresh basil if you have it (optional)

Cook all the pasta per packet instructions. While it boils: spray oil in a pan, fry garlic and chilli flakes on medium heat for 1 min (don't burn the garlic!!). Pour in tomatoes, add sugar and salt, simmer 10 mins. Drain pasta, toss through sauce.

**For Tuesday dinner:** eat half, hot, from a bowl, feeling Italian.
**For Wednesday lunch:** other half straight into tupperware. Lid on, fridge. Eat cold tomorrow — the chilli mellows overnight and it's genuinely lovely cold.

### 🌿 SUMMER VEGGIE PASTA (Wednesday Dinner → Thursday Lunch)

**Makes 2 portions (dinner + lunch tub):**
- 200g dried pasta (any shape)
- Spray oil
- 1 courgette — halved lengthways then sliced into half-moons
- About 10 cherry tomatoes — halved
- 2 cloves garlic — sliced
- Juice of 1 lemon
- Salt and pepper
- Handful of fresh basil or rocket if you have it

Cook all the pasta. While it boils: spray oil in a pan, fry courgette on medium-high 5 mins until golden. Add cherry tomatoes and garlic, cook 2-3 more mins until tomatoes soften. Drain pasta, toss through veg, squeeze lemon over, season.

**For Wednesday dinner:** eat half hot with basil on top.
**For Thursday lunch:** other half into tupperware with an extra squeeze of lemon. Even better cold — the lemon keeps it fresh.

### 🌮 MEXICAN RICE BOWL (Thursday Dinner → Friday Lunch)

**Makes 2 portions (dinner + lunch tub):**
- 150g dry rice (about a mugful — makes loads when cooked!!)
- 1 tin black beans (400g) — drained and rinsed
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- Juice of 1 lime
- 6-8 cherry tomatoes — roughly chopped
- Quarter of a cucumber — diced
- 2 spring onions — sliced
- Small handful of fresh coriander if you have it — roughly chopped
- 3-4 tbsp salsa from a jar
- Salt

Cook rice per packet instructions. Warm beans in a small pan with cumin, paprika, lime juice, and a splash of water — just 3-4 mins. Chop all the fresh bits while things cook.

**For Thursday dinner:** half the rice in a bowl, half the beans on top, pile on the fresh bits, spoon over salsa.
**For Friday lunch:** same in a tupperware. The rice absorbs the bean juices overnight and it's the BEST leftover on the entire list. You will look forward to this one.

### 🥙 BAKED FALAFEL PITTAS (Friday Dinner)

**Makes 1-2 portions:**
- 4-6 frozen falafel (check packet for baking instructions — usually 15-20 mins at 200°C)
- 2 pitta breads — last of the pack from Saturday!!
- Handful of lettuce — shredded
- 4-5 cherry tomatoes — halved
- Quarter cucumber — sliced
- A few slices of red onion
- Juice of half a lemon
- Hot sauce to taste

Bake falafel per packet. Warm pittas in toaster. Split pittas open, stuff with salad, add falafel, squeeze lemon, hot sauce on top. 

**Fat:** check falafel packet — baked is much lower than fried. Aim for under 5g fat per serving.
