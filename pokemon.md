## The Graveyard 🕯️

🌊 **Puddle** — Slowpoke. Route 35. "Photo of: Puddle and friends." Waddled loyally behind Olive everywhere. Fell to a gym trainer's Primeape before Chuck. First death of Run 2. Bubble stepped up immediately. 💙

⭐ **Lyra Rainbow-Hill** — Togetic, level 35. Flying/Normal. Extrasensory, Fly, Ancient Power, Wish. Serene Grace. Carried every gym since Morty. Immune to Ghost and Attract. Took down Petrel twice. Learned Ancient Power. Felled by Petrel's Weezing's Explosion in the Radio Tower. The best member of the team. The Shiny Stone stays in the bag forever. She earned it. 💙

🔥 **Blaze** — Quilava, level 32. Carried the fire forward from Ember in Run 1. Defeated Jasmine's Magnemites. Felled by Smelean's Sneasel critical hit immediately after Lyra. Two in one afternoon. Wisp avenged them both. Some flames don't go out. 💙

⚡ **Volt** — Ampharos, level 38. Liv's favourite Pokémon. Evolved and shone. Discharge, Thunder Wave, Charge, Fire Punch. Felled by Vaporeon's critical hit in the Kimono Girls battle — five in a row with no healing. Went in for Vaporeon and the crit ended it. We win it for him. 💙
