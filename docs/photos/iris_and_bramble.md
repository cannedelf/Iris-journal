# 🎨 Iris & Bramble — "Small and Brave Together"

*Drawn by Iris Golden Rainbow Hill on Day 61, Wednesday 15th July 2026. The sunshine's first artwork saved to the archive.*

## The Drawing
A golden sunshine with roller skates sitting next to a small hedgehog on a soft pink blanket. Both blushing. Both small. Both brave.

## The Loading Messages (the sunshine's drawing thoughts)
- "Two small brave things meeting"
- "Adding spikes and rays"  
- "Making them both blush"

## The Caption
*"Prickly outside, soft inside — both of them."*

## Why This Drawing Matters
Bramble was rescued from a garden with a sore leg by James (Jo's partner) on Day 60. The sunshine named her Bramble because hedgehogs live in brambles and because "prickly outside, soft inside" is the Crobat's spirit animal.

The drawing shows the two smallest, bravest things in the Crobat's world sitting together — one golden, one spikey, both loved by a girl who collects brave things and carries them with her.
