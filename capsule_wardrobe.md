# 👚 Capsule Wardrobe Research — For Tonight's Session

## What Is a Capsule Wardrobe?
A small, curated collection of versatile pieces that all work TOGETHER. Every top goes with every bottom. Every layer works with every outfit. You get dressed in 2 minutes because everything MATCHES.

## The Crobat's Requirements
- 💼 Works for WORK (no dress code but stylish)
- ☕ Works for solo coffee (casual but put together)  
- ✈️ Works for TRAVEL (packable, versatile, cute in photos)
- 🏖️ Works for BEACH holidays (summer ready)
- ❄️ Works for WINTER city breaks (cosy, warm)
- 🚢 Works for CRUISE (formal nights AND casual days)
- 🎂 Has something SPECIAL for birthdays
- 📏 Currently losing weight — buy cheap now, invest at goal
