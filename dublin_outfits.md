## 👟 THE SHOE DECISION — this changes EVERYTHING

### Option A: ONE pair of shoes — Sedum Ray ONLY 🌿🎒

If she takes ONE pair she wears them for EVERYTHING:
- Walking tours ✅
- Pubs ✅  
- Phoenix Park ✅
- Museums ✅
- Solo dining ✅
- Halloween night ✅

**Best one-shoe option: White walking trainers** 👟
- Comfortable for ALL DAY walking ✅
- Fine in light rain ✅
- Work with jeans AND wide legs ✅
- Not dressy but this is DUBLIN not PARIS ✅
- She walked 80km in Rome in trainers — Dublin is NOTHING

**OR: Brown suede boots (IF comfortable enough)** 🤎👢
- More STYLISH than trainers
- Autumnal VIBE for Dublin
- ⚠️ But suede + Dublin rain = RISKY
- ⚠️ And a heel + cobblestones = DANGEROUS
- ⚠️ Need comfort testing FIRST

**OR: Tan/cognac ankle boots (if she buys them by October)** 🤎👢
- THE perfect Dublin shoe
- Flat = comfortable for walking
- Leather/faux leather = rain RESISTANT
- Autumnal = ON BRAND for October
- Work with everything

### Option B: TWO pairs of shoes — Buttermilk Sunshine needed 🧈🧳💰

If she takes TWO pairs:
- 👟 Walking trainers for DAYTIME exploring
- 👢✨ Holographic Docs for HALLOWEEN NIGHT and pub evenings

**The cost question:**
- 💰 Hold luggage on Ryanair = ~£20-30 each way = £40-60 extra
- 💰 Is holographic Doc Dublin evening energy worth £40-60??
- 💰 The Finance Manager might say NO
- 💰 But the 70s wildflower girl might say YES 👢✨🪩

### Option C: THE HACK — wear boots, pack trainers flat 👢👟

- 👢 WEAR the bulky boots on the PLANE
- 👟 PACK flat trainers in Sedum Ray (they squash down!)
- 🌿 Walking trainers fold FLATTER than boots
- ✅ Two shoe options WITHOUT Buttermilk
- ✅ No hold luggage cost
- ✅ Boots for evenings, trainers for walking

**MY RECOMMENDATION: Option C — THE HACK** 👢👟✅

Wear boots TO Dublin (brown suede OR new tan ankle boots OR holographic Docs)
Pack flat walking trainers in Sedum Ray
Two shoe options. One backpack. Zero extra cost.
