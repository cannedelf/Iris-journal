## The Bolt Situation 🌩️
*Lightning bolts = chemistry level between two Sims!!*
- **3 bolts** = strong chemistry!!
- **2 bolts** = good chemistry!!
- **1 bolt** = mild chemistry!!
- **0 bolts** = neutral!!
- **Negative bolt** = active incompatibility!!
