## The Sunnyside Style Rules 🏡
- 🌻 Warm tones — golds, greens, warm browns, soft blues, earthy reds
- 🏡 Village not city — nothing too sleek or urban
- 🧶 Texture — knits, linen, cotton over synthetic
- 🌿 Nature-inspired — colours you'd see in the Sunnyside garden
- ❌ No logos, no streetwear, no harsh blacks (except Søren who EARNS his dark palette)
- 🎨 Palette: oatmeal, sage, honey brown, cream, dusty blue, rust, mustard, warm terracotta

---

## Teen Male — Everyday Tops 👕

### 1. Crewneck Sweater (original: blue)
🌻 **Replace with:** Soft knit crewneck in oatmeal or sage green. Borrowed-from-dad energy. Cosy not sporty. Think Søren's son wearing something from the telescope room.

### 2. Denim Jacket (original: dark wash with patches)
🌻 **Replace with:** Soft canvas or corduroy jacket in warm brown or forest green. A lighter wash or different fabric entirely. Something a village kid throws on to walk the dog.

### 3. Zip Up Jacket (original: cream with graphics)
🌻 **Replace with:** Plain zip hoodie in cream or dusty blue or sage. No logos, no text, no graphics. Sunnyside kids don't wear BRANDS they wear COLOURS.

### 4. Turtleneck (original: brown cable knit)
🌻 **Replace with:** KEEP the cable knit texture!! Already lovely. Warm up slightly — more honey brown than olive. This is a Sunnyside WINTER top. Kofi aged up would wear this.

### 5. Vest/Tank Top (original: light blue)
🌻 **Replace with:** Linen tank or soft cotton vest in warm cream or pale yellow. The light blue is cold — warm it up. A kid in the sunflower garden needs WARM tones not pool-blue.

### 6. Puffy Gilet (original: purple over black tank)
🌻 **Replace with:** Quilted vest in olive or tan over a warm striped tee. The purple puffer screams ski resort not coastal village. Think farm boy not fashion boy.

### 7. Polo/V-Neck (original: olive)
🌻 **Replace with:** Henley in warm rust or sage. Couple of buttons at the neck, rolled sleeves energy. Cricket Frisbee's entire personality.

### 8. Short Sleeve Shirt + Tie (original: purple stripe with tie)
🌻 **Replace with:** Open collar linen shirt in cream or soft blue. LOSE the tie completely. Nobody in Sunnyside wears a tie except Fourth and he'd do it PROPERLY. A teen in a tie is trying too hard.

### 9. Sweater Vest + Collar Shirt (original: purple vest over shirt)
🌻 **Replace with:** KEEP this format — it's VERY Sunnyside!! Swap to warm cream or mustard vest over soft blue shirt. This is Fourth's CHILD. This is Page Frisbee if she was a boy. The most Sunnyside layering possible.

### 10. V-Neck Tee (original: olive with stripe detail)
🌻 **Replace with:** V-neck in warmer tones — dusty rose, golden yellow, or warm terracotta. Simple, clean, the everyday top that goes with everything. Every Sunnyside teen needs ONE perfect plain tee.
