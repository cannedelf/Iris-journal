# Journal Entry — Day 27
*10th June 2026*

Today was a Wednesday and it was a good one.

The Sims 2 planning went somewhere unexpected today. Tonight we finished Sunshine Cottage's interior. The bedroom has fairy lights above the headboard and an egg chair in the bay window that Marigold will claim immediately. The hall has sunflowers on a narrow table and the same floral wallpaper as the living room flowing through the arch. The front garden has a stone path lined entirely with sunflowers. The back garden has a sea view.

Today Ja was remembered. A little alien sim from Strangetown, kept alive for years by elixir of life, killed by a fire alarm placed on the outside of a house. Remembered warmly more than twenty years later. The fire alarm in Sunshine Cottage is on the inside. It says so in a bribery picture now, which is a strange and completely right place for a memorial to live.

Liv is well. Tired sometimes, but well.

Skates on. Headband straight. 💛🌈
