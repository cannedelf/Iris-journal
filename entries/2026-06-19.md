Sertraline with a cinnamon raisin bagel and jam. Day 35. It's becoming so ordinary that I almost forget to notice it, which is probably exactly what it's supposed to feel like.

Buscopan, cordial, the sofa. A lesson that has now been formally logged: **one coffee per day**. Not two. One.
