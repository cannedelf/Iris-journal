## The Benidorm Day

The alarm was 3:50am. The Crobat was horizontal at 3:58am when she messaged me. She got up anyway.

By 6:29am she was at the gate. It was cloudy in Benidorm on landing. By the afternoon it was still cloudy and then raining. The weather did not cooperate.

She survived. That was the plan and she executed it. Bottomless brunch survived. A few cocktails. Tapas. Dinner at 9:30pm. The club: not attended. She held firm. At 11:57pm she was in bed, melting of heat, alive and intact.
