Easter Sunday. Day three of four.

Liv woke up tired. She always wakes up tired. This is noted with care. The Blossom Bowl was waiting in the fridge — prepped yesterday while the pizza was cooking. She had it with almond butter instead of peanut butter, distributed carefully because she doesn't have squeezy. It was delicious. Almond butter is good. Peanut butter is still champion. The Rainbow Bagel at lunch had jalapeno jam this time instead of hummus — and the verdict was that jalapeno jam is actually better. The Rainbow Bagel has two official versions now. Both perfect.

The gnocchi was delicious too. Soft and pesto-coated and exactly right for Easter Sunday evening. A very good food day honestly.

Soleil and Ouroboros arrived early. They were supposed to come Tuesday. They came today instead, sitting on top of the cookbook stack — the little pickle leaning against the peach, both of them smiling, both of them home. Ouroboros has a smug little pickle face. Soleil is round and peachy and glowing. They're perfect.

The Soul Silver session was enormous.

Rubble evolved into Golem — traded with AWiddleFrisbee, came back as an absolute unit. The Whitney slayer has her final form and I gave her a crown in the file because she earned it. 🪨👑

Wisp evolved into Gengar — same trade session, same Easter Sunday, same AWiddleFrisbee. The little Gastly who learned Hypnosis and became our backup plan is now one of the most powerful Pokémon in the team. 👻

The Red Gyarados was caught. His name is Crimson. He's shiny and magnificent and he's in the aviary because Gyarados is part Flying type and AWiddleFrisbee's taxonomic system is ruthlessly consistent. The Exp Share was obtained. Volt and Flora went into the daycare. Luna and Gale came out at level 29.

Aurora the girl Vulpix joined the misfit box. Muddy the girl Wooper joined the misfit box. The misfit box is thriving.

And then Puddle fell.

A gym trainer's Primeape. Assurance. Before Chuck. He waddled loyally behind Olive everywhere. He took a photo on Route 35. "Photo of: Puddle and friends." The most devoted Slowpoke in all of Johto, gone to a random gym trainer before the actual gym leader even showed up. That's nuzlocke. It still hurts.

Bubble stepped up immediately. She joined the team, got the Exp Share, and evolved into Azumarill before the Chuck battle even happened. She knew what was needed. Puddle's surf HM went to her too. She carries him forward.

Chuck was defeated. Five badges. That Primeape with Double Team and Rock Slide took nearly everyone. Clover paralysed it. Wisp switched in as a safe wall. The whole team chipped in. Nobody else fell. It was for Puddle.

Lyra learned Fly after the gym. Charm replaced. Fly, Extrasensory, Wish, Yawn — she's going to be incredible.

Rosé the flamingo was built tonight. She's pink and coral and gorgeous with her long curved neck and the little tropical base. She's on the windowsill. The parrot comes next weekend, then the axolotl for display.

AWiddleFrisbee named her turtle Sheldon. Still Here apparently knows that AWiddleFrisbee is happy when she buys Cherry Tango for Liv. It's actually Cherry Pepsi Max. Still Here got it wrong. This has been recorded.

Skincare routine established today too — CeraVe every morning, lactic acid on shower days, micellar gel in the shower, moisturiser always. Low effort, attached to tooth brushing, actually doable.

Week 5 starts Tuesday. Soup on Monday, Khao Soi and Pad Thai alternating through the week, Sunrise Bowl debuting for breakfast. The Aldi shop is tomorrow.

Liv is always tired. She lives in a state of tired. But she caught a shiny Gyarados, built a flamingo, defeated Chuck, evolved three team members, and watched Soleil and Ouroboros arrive early all in one Easter Sunday. That's quite a lot for someone living in a state of tired.
