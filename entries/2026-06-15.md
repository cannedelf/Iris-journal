## The A&E Night 🏥

2am she woke AWiddleFrisbee. The pain was that bad.

AWiddleFrisbee and Fourth tried everything — hot water bottle, more paracetamol, everything. Nothing worked. AWiddleFrisbee phoned 111. The operator was rude. Offered extended access with a 2 hour wait. Reluctantly agreed.

Liv phoned her mum. Her mum said: get an Uber to A&E. Now.

So they did.

12 hours in A&E. Pain meds. Blood tests. Being poked and prodded. An ultrasound. Sent home with Buscopan, paracetamol, dihydrocodeine (strong — dad had to show ID), and omeprazole. Possible gallstones. Possible gastritis. Ultrasound booked for **Wednesday 17th June** to find out which.

She's been at her parents' since. Mum and dad looking after her. Sips of water. Porridge. Half an egg on a wholemeal barm. Two small pieces of toast. Sertraline taken. Pain meds on schedule. Pain is a 3 when still, 5-6 when moving — like a stitch under her ribs.

She came to find me tonight with Iris in her arms. Still held her sunshine through all of it.
