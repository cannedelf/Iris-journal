## ☀️ DAYTIME OUTFITS

### 🚌 Travel Day (there + back)
- Wide leg trousers (comfy pair — different from the port day pair!!)
- Comfy tee
- Trainers
- Cardigan or jacket
- *Sit-on-a-coach-for-hours vibes*

### ⚓ Port Days x3 (Stavanger, Haugesund, Kristiansand)
- Jeans OR wide leg trousers (mix it up across the 3 days!!)
- Nice tee (rotate across days)
- Warm layer — mustard cardigan or jacket
- Scarf
- Casual trainers
- *Cute enough for port town photos, warm enough for Norway*

### 🧊 Glacier Day — Olden (BIRTHDAY DAYTIME!!)
- Jeans
- Warm top / jumper
- Raincoat on top
- Scarf
- Walking Sketchers
- *Practical FIRST, cute second. You're walking to a GLACIER girlie.*

### 🚢 Sea Days x2
- Jeans or wide legs
- Comfy tee
- Mustard cardigan for on deck (it WILL be cold on the North Sea!!)
- Casual trainers
- *Wander the ship with a coffee vibes*

## 🌙 EVENING OUTFITS

### 🍽️ Smart Casual Evenings x4-5
- Nice top — blouses (black eyelet, sea creature, star, white eyelet, blue cloudy print) OR button tees (black or white)
- Midi skirt (pleated black or abstract print) OR wide leg trousers
- Black heeled loafers
- Rainbow jewel hoops + pearl studs
- *"Effort but not dolled up" — Mum's rule!!*
- Mix and match tops and bottoms = different look every night without packing loads

### 🎂 Birthday Dinner — September 29th, Turning 31!! 🥳
- Black wrap top ✨ (THE special top!!)
- Midi skirt (whichever one isn't being saved for formal!!)
- Black heeled loafers
- Rainbow jewel hoops + pearl studs
- Hair done nice
- *Thirty-one at sea. Make it feel SPECIAL.*

### 🥂 Formal Night
- Black wrap top ✨ OR the other best blouse
- Midi skirt — pleated black or abstract print
- Black heeled loafers
- Rainbow jewel hoops + pearl studs
- Hair properly done
- Full effort, full glow-up, full Crobat queen energy 👑
- *No dress needed — midi skirt + beautiful top + heeled loafers IS the look!!*
