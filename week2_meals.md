## 🇬🇷 1. ORZO SALAD — *2 portions*
**Sat dinner + Sun lunch. Gets BETTER overnight.** 🧊

### Ingredients
- 150g orzo (dry)
- 1 pepper, diced
- ¼ cucumber, diced
- 10 cherry tomatoes, halved
- ½ red onion, finely diced
- 60g black olives, sliced
- Small handful fresh parsley, chopped
- **1 lemon — juice AND zest**
- 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
- **1 tsp olive oil ONLY** 🪨
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- ½ tsp salt
- ¼ tsp black pepper
- **Pinch MSG**

### Method
1. Boil the orzo per pack (~8 mins). **Drain and rinse under COLD water** — this stops it clumping. Drain WELL.
2. While it cooks: dice the pepper, cucumber, red onion. Halve the tomatoes. Slice the olives. Chop the parsley.
3. **The dressing:** in the bottom of a big bowl whisk together the lemon juice + zest, red wine vinegar, 1 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, salt, pepper, MSG. *(The acid does the work — that's why it only needs 1 tsp of oil.)* 🍋
4. Tip the cold orzo in. Stir so every grain gets coated.
5. Add all the veg, olives and parsley. Fold through.
6. Taste. More lemon? More salt? More MSG?
7. **Half into a tub for Sunday.** 📦

**Fat: ~5g per portion**

## 🫘 2. BUTTERBEAN STEW — *4 portions!!*
**The Week 2 chilli. Sun dinner + Mon lunch + Tue dinner + Wed lunch.** 📦📦
**LIQUID SMOKE IS THE SECRET** 🔥

### Ingredients
- **2 × 400g tins butter beans**, drained & rinsed
- **2 × 400g tins chopped tomatoes**
- 1 onion, diced
- 4 garlic cloves, crushed
- 2 peppers, chopped chunky
- 1 tbsp tomato purée
- **2 tsp smoked paprika**
- **½ tsp liquid smoke** 🔥 *(START HERE — it's POWERFUL. Taste before adding more.)*
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- 1 stock cube + 200ml boiling water
- 1 tsp sugar
- Juice of ½ lemon (at the end!)
- ½ tsp salt, black pepper
- **Pinch MSG**
- Fresh parsley to serve
- Spray oil 🪨
- **Crusty bread for dunking** 🍞

### Method
1. Spray a big pan. Soften the onion 5 mins. Add garlic + peppers, 3 more mins.
2. Add the tomato purée, smoked paprika, oregano, thyme. Stir 1 min — **let the spices toast**, it wakes them up.
3. Tins of tomatoes in. Stock in. Sugar in. Salt, pepper, MSG.
4. **Butter beans in.** Stir gently — don't smash them.
5. **Liquid smoke: ½ tsp. STIR. TASTE.** Add more only if it needs it. 🔥
6. **Simmer 25-30 mins** uncovered, stirring now and then. It should go THICK and glossy, not watery.
7. **Lemon juice at the END** — off the heat. It lifts the whole thing. 🍋
8. Parsley on top. Crusty bread.
9. **THREE portions into tubs.** 📦📦📦 It gets better every single day.

**Fat: ~2g per portion** 🪨

## 🍚 3. VEGGIE PAELLA-ISH RICE — *2 portions*
**Mon dinner + Tue lunch.** 📦

### Ingredients
- 150g rice (dry)
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 garlic cloves, crushed
- 2 peppers (red + yellow), sliced
- 150g mushrooms, sliced
- 100g frozen peas
- 1 × 400g tin butter beans, drained
- 1 × 400g tin chopped tomatoes
- **2 tsp smoked paprika**
- **½ tsp turmeric** *(for the GOLD)* ☀️
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 stock cube + 400ml boiling water
- ½ tsp salt, black pepper
- **Pinch MSG**
- **1 lemon, cut into wedges to serve** 🍋
- Fresh parsley
- Spray oil 🪨

### Method
1. Spray a wide pan. Onion 5 mins. Garlic + peppers 3 mins. **Mushrooms 4 mins** — let them go golden, don't crowd them.
2. Smoked paprika + turmeric + oregano. **Stir 1 min to toast.** ☀️
3. **Rice in — stir so every grain gets coated** in the spices.
4. Tin of tomatoes + stock. Salt, pepper, MSG. Stir ONCE.
5. **Simmer 15 mins with a lid on. DON'T STIR IT.** *(That's what makes paella paella — the bottom goes slightly crispy.)*
6. Peas + butter beans on top. Lid back on. **5 more mins.**
7. Off the heat, lid on, **rest 5 mins.**
8. Fork it through. Parsley. **Big squeeze of lemon.** 🍋
9. **Half in a tub.** 📦

**Fat: ~3g per portion**

## 🍜 4. STIR FRY + RICE — *2 portions*
**Wed dinner + Thu lunch.** 📦 *(Rice NOT noodles — noodles go soggy in a tub!!)*

### Ingredients
- 150g rice (dry)
- **300g frozen stir fry veg mix** 🧊 *(cheaper, zero waste!!)*
- 150g mushrooms, sliced
- 100g frozen edamame
- 3 garlic cloves, crushed
- 2 tsp ground ginger *(or 1 thumb fresh, grated)*
- 2 spring onions, sliced
- **3 tbsp soy sauce**
- **1 tbsp honey** 🍯
- 2 tbsp sweet chilli sauce
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar *(or lemon juice)*
- **1 tsp sesame seeds** 🌰
- **Pinch MSG**
- Spray oil 🪨

### Method
1. **Rice on first** — 12-15 mins.
2. **The sauce:** mix soy, honey, sweet chilli, rice vinegar, MSG in a mug. Set aside.
3. Spray a wok, get it **HOT.** Mushrooms in first — 4 mins until golden.
4. Garlic + ginger — **30 seconds**, don't burn it. 🔥
5. **Frozen veg straight in from the freezer.** 4-5 mins, keep it moving, until hot through and still with a bit of BITE.
6. Edamame in. 2 mins.
7. **Sauce in.** Toss for 1 min until everything's glossy and coated.
8. Rice in the bowl, stir fry on top. Spring onions + sesame seeds. 🌰
9. **Half in a tub.** 📦

**Fat: ~4g per portion**

## 🍅 5. BRUSCHETTA 👑 — *2 portions*
**Thu dinner + Fri lunch.** **THE BEST ONE. Appears in BOTH weeks. It EARNED it.** 🍅

### Ingredients
- 4 thick slices tiger bloomer 🍞
- **10 cherry tomatoes — RED, ORANGE AND YELLOW** *(a sunset on toast!!)* 🔴🟠🟡
- ¼ red onion, finely diced
- 1 garlic clove, **cut in half**
- Handful fresh basil, torn
- **½ lemon, juiced**
- **1 tsp olive oil** 🪨
- **Balsamic glaze to drizzle** 🫒 *(THE UPGRADE)*
- Harissa, to streak 🌶️
- ½ tsp salt, black pepper
- **Pinch MSG**

### Method
1. **THE TOMATO MIX FIRST:** dice the tomatoes, finely dice the red onion, tear the basil. Squeeze in the lemon **through your fingers** *(catches the seeds!!)*. Salt, pepper, MSG. Stir.
2. **LET IT SIT 10 MINUTES.** The juices MINGLE. This is the whole trick. ⏰
3. **The bread:** thick slices, drizzle with olive oil, under the grill.
4. **WATCH IT.** 🔥👀 The window between GOLDEN and BURNT is about 30 seconds. Flip it — the second side is faster.
5. **THE MAGIC STEP:** the second it's out, **rub the cut garlic clove over the hot toast.** The heat pulls the flavour into the bread. 🧄
6. Pile the tomato mix HIGH.
7. **Drizzle the balsamic glaze.** 🫒
8. Streak of harissa if you want HEAT. 🌶️
9. **EAT IMMEDIATELY.**

📦 **FOR THE TUB:** tomato mix in ONE tub, **bread SEPARATE.** Toast at work, assemble at the desk. **NEVER pre-assemble — it goes soggy.**

**Fat: ~5g per portion**

## 🌯 6. FAJITAS — *1 portion, Friday treat*

### Ingredients
- **2 wraps**
- **100g frozen Quorn chicken-style pieces** 🧊 *(~2g fat per 100g — one of the safest proteins!!)*
- ½ × 400g tin black beans, drained
- 1 pepper, sliced
- ½ onion, sliced
- 100g mushrooms, sliced
- **1 tsp smoked paprika**
- 1 tsp cumin
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- ½ tsp dried oregano
- **½ lime, juiced** 🍋
- 2 tbsp salsa 🫙
- Shredded lettuce 🥬
- ½ tsp salt, black pepper
- **Pinch MSG**
- Spray oil 🪨

**NO cheese. NO sour cream.** 🪨 *(The mission.)* 📉

### Method
1. Spray a pan, get it hot. Onion + pepper — 5 mins, let them **CHAR a bit** at the edges. That's the fajita flavour. 🔥
2. Mushrooms — 4 mins.
3. **Quorn pieces straight from frozen** — 5-6 mins until hot through.
4. Smoked paprika, cumin, garlic powder, oregano, salt, pepper, MSG. Stir 1 min.
5. Black beans in. 2 mins to warm through.
6. **Lime juice off the heat.** 🍋
7. Warm the wraps. Load: lettuce, filling, salsa.

**Fat: ~6g per portion**
