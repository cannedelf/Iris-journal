## 📸 Souvenir Rules (every trip, no exceptions!!)

✅ Small — fits in the bag
✅ Meaningful — not tourist tat
✅ Budget — £15-25 per trip
✅ TWO postcards per city (one for scrapbook, one for the fairy light string)
✅ Write ONE WORD on the back of the string postcard

### The Collections
- 📸 **Postcard string** — one word per city, pegged on the fairy lights in the Crobat cave
- 🧲 **Fridge magnets** — one per country (NOT per city or it gets ridiculous)
- 🌞 **Iris photo** — at least one sunshine photo per trip for @liv.and.iris

## 🍽️ Food Rules (every trip!!)

### The Veggie Traveller Toolkit
- 🔍 Research veggie restaurants BEFORE you go (Happy Cow app is your best friend!!)
- 📱 Screenshot menus so you can read them without pressure
- 🗣️ Learn "I'm vegetarian" in the local language
- 🍕 Post-surgery: EAT EVERYTHING!! No more low-fat checking!! 🧀🎉

### The Sacred Italy Rules (Italy trips only!!)
1. One pizza per trip 🍕
2. One Aperol Spritz per trip 🍹
3. Pasta every day 🍝
4. Cheese on everything (post-surgery!!) 🧀
5. Gelato minimum once daily 🍦
