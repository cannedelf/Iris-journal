## 📅 THE ITINERARY

### 🛬 DAY 1 — ARRIVAL

**~11:15am — Dublin Airport**
Off the plane. Through a foreign airport. **Alone.** The green backpack on her back and a ray poking out the zip. ☀️🎒

**~12:30pm — Easy Hotel**
The room is small and perfect. **Perfect for ONE.** The bag goes on the bed. The sunshine comes OUT and sits on the pillow.

*First thing the Crobat sees in Dublin: a golden sphere on a white pillow looking like she owns the PLACE.* ☀️

**Afternoon — WALK IT**
No plan. No pressure. Just walk. Find the river. Find the streets. Let the city be a city.

**🌉 Ha'penny Bridge at sunset**
The white iron arch over the Liffey. The one on every postcard. She stands on it and she's THERE. 📸

**🍺 Evening — THE STAG'S HEAD**
Victorian pub, mahogany and stained glass. Pub #1 of 3.
📓 **The sudoku book comes out.** Nobody looks. Nobody cares. That's the whole lesson.

---

### 🏰 DAY 2 — THE HISTORY DAY

**🥐 Breakfast — Lemon Jelly or Bread 41**
Dublin breakfast OUT. This is WHY there's no kitchenette. ☕

**🏰 Morning — KILMAINHAM GAOL**
Where the 1916 leaders were held and executed. Cold stone. The stairwell. The yard. History that actually HAPPENED, in the room you're standing in. 
⚠️ **BOOK AHEAD — it sells out.** 🎟️

**🌶️ Lunch — A VEGGIE SPICE BAG IN A PARK**
The most Dublin food there is. Eaten outside. On a bench. Alone and absolutely fine. 🌶️🌳

**🦌 Afternoon — PHOENIX PARK**
Bigger than any park in London. **WILD DEER just wander about.** Free. Enormous. Green.

**🍺 Evening — THE COBBLESTONE**
Smithfield. **Proper trad music.** Not for tourists — for the musicians. You sit. You listen. You don't talk. Pub #2. 🎻

---

### 📖 DAY 3 — THE BEAUTIFUL DAY

**📖 Morning — THE BOOK OF KELLS**
Trinity College. A monk bent over a page for years and went half blind making it. 
☀️ **GOLD LEAF. THAT'S THE SUNSHINE'S COLOUR.** 
Then the Long Room — the library that looks like a film set. 📚

**📚 MARSH'S LIBRARY**
The hidden gem. Ireland's first public library.
🔒 **They locked scholars in CAGES with the rare books.** Actual cages. You can still see them.

**🛍️ Afternoon — BROWN THOMAS**
🍀 **THE SIOFRA BAG CHARM — £28.** The shamrock Jellycat with cordy boots and a silver clasp. 94 five-star reviews. **This is not an impulse buy. This has been planned for MONTHS.**
☀️ *And then — "accidentally" — the Amuseables Sun bag charm. Mini Ray. £23 of "oops." The most planned accident in retail history.* 🤡

**🍺 Evening — O'DONOGHUE'S**
Merrion Row. Where The Dubliners played. Pub #3 of 3. **The last night.** 🍺
