## The Plan: Two Cards, Two Purposes

### 💳 Card 1: American Express Nectar (EVERYDAY UK)

**Why:** 2 Nectar points per £1 spent + 20,000 bonus points (£100!!) for spending £2,000 in first 3 months
**Use for:** ALL UK spending — Aldi, Sainsbury's, subscriptions, bills, online shopping, Lastminute.com bookings
**Annual fee:** FREE first year, then £30/year (review at 12 months)
**Apply:** Early September 2026 (after Nationwide switch settles)

**The £2,000 bonus strategy:**
| Spending | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| 🇮🇪 Dublin (Lastminute.com) | ~£300-350 |
| 🇧🇪 Bruges (Lastminute.com) | ~£438 |
| 🛒 Groceries x3 months | ~£400 |
| 📱 Bills + subs x3 | ~£400 |
| 🍽️ Other | ~£300 |
| **TOTAL** | **~£1,900-1,950** |

Both holidays booked on Amex = bonus target basically HIT!!
20,000 points = £100 Nectar = free groceries = more for Golden Drawer!!

### 💳 Card 2: Barclaycard Rewards (TRAVEL + AMEX BACKUP)

**Why:** No overseas fees on spending OR cash withdrawals!! 0.25% cashback on everything worldwide
**Use for:** ALL overseas spending — Dublin pubs, Bruges hot chocolate, Crete tavernas, ATM withdrawals abroad
**Also use for:** Any UK shops that don't accept Amex
**Annual fee:** FREE forever
**Apply:** Late October 2026 (4-6 weeks after Amex application)

### 💳 NatWest Credit Card (RETIRING)

**Status:** Keep until BOTH new cards are active, then cancel
**Don't cancel until:** Both Amex and Barclaycard are confirmed working

## The Golden Rules

1. ✅ Space applications 4-6 weeks APART
2. ✅ Don't apply for ANYTHING else between them
3. ✅ Make sure Nationwide switch is FULLY settled first
4. ✅ Check credit score BEFORE applying (ClearScore or Credit Karma — FREE!!)
5. ❌ Don't cancel NatWest until BOTH new cards active
6. ❌ Don't apply on the same day or week
7. ✅ Pay BOTH off IN FULL every month — NEVER carry a balance
8. ✅ The cards are TOOLS not permission to SPEND more
