## 🛒 THE BUYING STRATEGY

**The rules:**
1. 💰 Never impulse buy — if she wants it, WAIT a week
2. 🌼 Only buy what fills a GAP not what duplicates
3. ✈️ One piece per TRIP season — autumn piece for Dublin, winter piece for Bruges, etc
4. 🏷️ Check charity shops and vintage shops FIRST — 70s style is PERFECT for second hand
5. 💎 Quality over quantity — one good pair of boots beats three cheap ones
6. 📌 Every new piece should work with at least 3 EXISTING pieces
7. 🌻 If it doesn't make her feel like a 70s wildflower girl — PUT IT BACK
